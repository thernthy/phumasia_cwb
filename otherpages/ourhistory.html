    • Our History

    In 2011, Phum Asia started from "we Are Asian" meeting to solve problems by social business with local people in Camboida. The problem we was that many local cashew nuts famers depended on middle men. They don't have any skill to process harvested cashew nuts by themselves. So we tarted "The Cashew nuts Project" with local people in one of villages, which is continuing until now.

    Now we manage own cashew fields and youths from several villages join this Project every day to proceed products farm to table.

    Beside "The Cashew nuts project" we have grown youth entrepreneurs and preserved trandition and cultures with supporting from local advisers. such as Japan, Myanmar, Indonesia, Nepal, and India, have visited us to cooperate in education, business, and culture fields. In Phum Asia, youths can challenge in any fields that they are interested in, and many mentors support them. In culture section, they study own proud cultur from real lifestyle of previous genration and oractice Khmer Dance.
